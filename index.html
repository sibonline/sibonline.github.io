Who We Are

The Student Initiative Board (SIB) is a student-led organisation dedicated to supporting the student body, encouraging creativity, and making school life more engaging and meaningful through student-run events and activities (Initiatives); where similar Initiatives are executed by our various Agencies.


Our Mission

  • To support the growth of student-led initiatives
  • To host innovative student events
  • To create a community of like-minded students around the globe

Our Agencies

Students Who Code (SWC)

A coding club for students passionate about technology. SWC organizes hackathons, robotics competitions, game jams, and workshops.

SIB MUN

Our flagship Model United Nations program empowers students to develop public speaking, diplomacy, negotiation, and leadership skills.

SIB Science

Encouraging scientific exploration and innovation, SIB Science engages students in experiments, research projects, science fairs, and sustainability initiatives.

SIB Community

A platform connecting students, fostering collaboration, events, workshops, and community-driven projects.
