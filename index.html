Our Clubs & Agencies

Students Who Code (SWC)

A coding club for students passionate about technology. SWC organizes hackathons, robotics competitions, game jams, and workshops. Members gain hands-on experience in programming, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Open to all students interested in coding and tech innovation.

Learn More

SIB MUN

Our flagship Model United Nations program empowers students to develop public speaking, diplomacy, negotiation, and leadership skills. Participants debate global issues in structured conferences.

Workshops, training sessions, and an annual MUN conference are hosted for students across schools.

Learn More

SIB Science

Encouraging scientific exploration and innovation, SIB Science engages students in experiments, research projects, science fairs, and sustainability initiatives. Members learn scientific methods, environmental stewardship, and collaborative project design.

The club fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and hands-on learning for students passionate about science and technology.
