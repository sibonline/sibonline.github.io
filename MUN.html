About SIB MUN

SIB MUN organizes Model United Nations conferences to empower students with public speaking, diplomacy, leadership, and negotiation skills. Participants represent countries and debate global issues in a structured format.

Activities & Programs

  • Workshops on public speaking and debate
  • Training sessions for committee simulation
  • Annual MUN conferences with multiple committees
  • Leadership and negotiation skill-building exercises
  • Collaboration with schools for inter-school MUN events

Join SIB MUN

Open to students interested in debate, diplomacy, and global affairs. Members develop leadership skills while engaging in structured debates and conferences.
