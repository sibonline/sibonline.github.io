About SIB Science

SIB Science inspires students to explore scientific concepts, conduct experiments, and engage in innovative projects. Members develop research skills, critical thinking, and environmental awareness.

Activities & Programs

  • Hands-on experiments and lab projects
  • Science fairs and exhibitions
  • Sustainability and environmental initiatives
  • Workshops on technology, waste management, and innovation
  • Collaborative research and peer mentorship

Join SIB Science

Open to students interested in science, innovation, and research. Members participate in projects that make a tangible impact while learning scientific methods and teamwork.
