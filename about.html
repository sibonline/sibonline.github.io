Our Mission

To create a culture of initiative, collaboration, and leadership by empowering students to bring their ideas to life and contribute meaningfully to the school community.

Our Structure

The SIB is currently run by a team of more than 10 members across different sections of Class 9, led from our organisational HQ in 9E. Each member takes on a specific role, ensuring every project is handled with care and precision. Additionally, our Ambassadors represent the Board in their respective sections, helping us stay connected with students across the grade.
