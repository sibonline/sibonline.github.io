Welcome to SIB Wiki

SIB Wiki is a collaborative encyclopedia inspired by Wikipedia. It is designed for students to create, explore, and share knowledge across various topics such as science, technology, history, and culture.

Every student can contribute by adding new pages, editing existing ones, and helping build a comprehensive knowledge base for everyone.

Features of SIB Wiki

Collaborative Editing

Edit pages directly from your browser. Students can contribute knowledge and improve content together.

Free Knowledge

All articles are freely accessible to everyone. Learn about a wide range of topics curated by students.

Structured Content

Pages are organized for easy navigation. Use the table of contents to find topics quickly.

Import & Export

Export your contributions as JSON or import content to easily manage pages and backups.

Contribute to SIB Wiki

Become part of our growing knowledge community. Add new articles, improve existing ones, and help SIB Wiki reach every student.
